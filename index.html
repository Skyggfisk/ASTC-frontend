What does this app do?

Find the shop it is selling the product your looking for with the best price possible. Find where that shop is in the map

How to use it

Type in the search bar what you want to find. The app will show the results that match your search sorted by price. If you don't know where the store selling that product is, simply click on the product and the app will show you where the store is.
